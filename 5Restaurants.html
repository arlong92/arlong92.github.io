My Foodie Five

Here are 5 of my favorite restaurants located in Houston, TX.

Chacho's

Chacho's is one of the best Tex-Mex restaurant chains in town. They always serve up large portions and great drinks. Try the shredded chicken quesadillas.

The Breakfast Klub

The Breakfast Klub is best known for their Sunday brunch, where they serve some of the best chicken wings & waffles I've ever had.

Mikki's Cafe

Seconds after you enter Mikki's (once you get past the long line), you'll notice their wall full of celebrity photos. No one leaves Houston without going to get a heaping helping of their delicious soul food. Don't miss their baked chicken and macaroni & cheese.

Pappasito's

Pappasito's is a seafood Tex-Mex fiesta. Their Plato Del Mar is to die for!

Pappadeaux

Another popular chain a part of the Pappas family of restaurants, Pappadeaux brings a fine dining feel to cajun cuisine. Their Pasta Mardi Gras is my favorite.
